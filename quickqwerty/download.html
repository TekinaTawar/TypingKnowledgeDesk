Online release

▶ Use QuickQWERTY online

The online release of QuickQWERTY can always be accessed at http://quickqwerty.com/.

The online release is usually a snapshot of the most recent developmental version of QuickQWERTY. Therefore, this release is usually more recent than the offline release. It may contain features and fixes that have not been tested much and will be made available in a future offline release after some testing.
